The next step on my journey is being stationed at Lajes AB, Azores Portugal which I leave in August this year for.

Hobbies and Interests

Triathlon

My first hobby is Triathlon and it definitely takes up the majority of my time. Growing up I always loved running and swimming. When I signed up and participated in my first race I was immediately hooked. Triathlon is an event that combines three disciplines; swimming, biking and running. There are several different distance races and it is an absolute great way to stay in shape.

I use several different websites to assist in the planning and tracking of progress with triathlon. They are linked below. The first is polar.com which is the website my watch syncs to which tracks all workouts and sleep. The second is the website for 80/20 triathlon where all my training plans come from. And finally the last is trifind.com which I use to search and register for races all over the country.
